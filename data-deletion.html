Data Deletion Instructions

UNECO AI · Effective July 10, 2026

You may request deletion of personal information associated with your communications with UNECO AI.

How to Submit a Request

  1. Email privacy@uneco.ai.
  2. Use the subject line Data Deletion Request.
  3. Include the phone number, email address, or account identifier used to communicate with UNECO.
  4. Describe the information you want deleted.

Verification

We may ask you to verify that you control the relevant account, phone number, or email address.

Processing

We will review verified requests and respond within a reasonable period. Some information may be retained when required by law or necessary for security and legitimate records.
